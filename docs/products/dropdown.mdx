# Dropdowns

Dropdowns are toggleable, contextual overlays for displaying lists of links

        <ui class="dropdown-menu">
            <li class="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 1</li>

            <li class="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 3</li>
        </ui>

## Menu alignment - Right

By default, a dropdown menu is automatically positioned 100% from the top and along the left side
of its parent. Add .dropdown-menu-right to a .dropdown-menu to right align the dropdown menu.

<div class="de-code">
    <div class="btn-group"> 
        <button class="btn btn-secondary btn-flat" role="button" data-toggle="dropdown">Dropdown Button<i class="dci dci-chevron-down"></i></button>
        <div class="dropdown-menu dropdown-menu-right">
            <button class="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 1</button>
            <button class="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 2</button>
            <button class="dropdown-item" role="button">Option 3</button>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
